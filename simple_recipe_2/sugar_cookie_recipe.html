Sugar Cookies


Ingredients

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven:Set your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients:In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cream butter & sugar:In a separate large bowl, beat the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add wet ingredients:Mix in the egg and vanilla until smooth.
  5. Combine:Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture and stir until a dough forms.
  6. Shape cookies:Roll dough into small balls (about 1 tablespoon each). Place on an ungreased baking sheet and flatten slightly with the bottom of a glass (you can dip it in sugar for a sparkly top).
  7. Bake:Bake for 8–10 minutes, or until edges are lightly golden.
  8. Cool:Let cookies sit on the baking sheet for 2 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
